Our Coaches - Alexander Beltre

Alexander Beltre is a USCF National Master and Senior Tournament Director. Before co-founding Chess Castle in 2011, Alexander Beltre was vice-president of programs for Chess-in-the-Schools. Alexander was part of the team that created a K-12 chess curriculum for NYC public schools and trained public school teachers in NYC, Miami and San Francisco in how to teach chess and run after school programs. Since 1998, Alexander Beltre has coached more than fifteen team and individual national champions from MS 118, PS70 and Mott Hall School.
